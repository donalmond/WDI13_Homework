Things Grace Hopper Did First.

  1. Functions in code. Once you've got a function working, you can reuse it over and over again.
  2. Meaningful words in code. Coding would be pretty weird without words that we can understand.
  3. Portability. She understood the limitations that would be imposed by some compilers almost getting the spec right but actually wrong.
  4. Its better to ask forgiveness than permission. Sometimes in life, often in code.
  5. The USS Hopper. I mean, its a thing, its got here name on it. Not a huge fan but whatever.
